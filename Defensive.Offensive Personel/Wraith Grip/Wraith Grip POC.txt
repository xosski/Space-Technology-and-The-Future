Title: Wraith Grip: Electrum-Tipped Hypercharge Disruption Round

Author: Quellaran Deluxethue Messat, Keeper of GhostCore Archives

Version: 0.1 (Combat Disruption Projectile for Multiphase Engagements)

Abstract: Wraith Grip is a directed-impact projectile system designed for spaceborne and atmospheric conflict. Utilizing a precision-forged electrum tip and a combustion-triggered hypercharge delivery core, Wraith Grip neutralizes modern armor systems by disrupting the energetic assumptions of the target's defense. The round is more than a munition: it is a tactical assertion of nullification. It ends not with rupture—but with silence.

1. Strategic Role & Intent

Wraith Grip exists to end standoffs. In high-tier conflicts—orbital, breach, or armored resistance—kinetic solutions often fail against adaptive or energy-diffuse shielding. Wraith Grip does not seek to pierce. It seeks to unbind the defense.

"If shielding is a song, this is the wrong note at the wrong time in the wrong key—so loud it forgets the lyrics."

The practical version would not “delete” matter, but it could break waste down into simpler components:

Waste → plasma / vapor / ions → sorted elements → reusable feedstock or safe exhaust

A good designation:

PAWS

Plasma Atomization Waste System

or more GhostCore:

VoidKiln

High-Energy Plasma Waste Reclamation Chamber

Core setup:

[PTEC Field Core] → geometric plasma throat → [PTEC Field Core]
                         ↑
                    waste injection

What it does:

Waste enters the chamber.
Shaped plasma fields compress it.
Heat + voltage ionize and break molecular bonds.
Magnetic/electric fields sort the resulting particles.
Useful material gets captured.
Unusable gas is filtered, neutralized, or vented.

The big win in space is not disposal — it’s reclamation.

You’d want to recover:

Carbon
Hydrogen
Oxygen
Nitrogen
Metals
Water vapor
Trace minerals

This connects directly to MFHAP:

VoidKiln / PAWS = breaks waste down
MFHAP = rebuilds useful material
PRISM Rail = routes heat, power, and recovered feedstock

So the ship becomes more closed-loop:

Waste goes in.
Structure, air, water, fuel, or feedstock comes back out.

The risks are serious though: toxic byproducts, radiation from extreme ionization, chamber erosion, magnetic failure, and accidental release of hot plasma. This would need containment layers, dump coils, emergency quench, and redundant filtration.

Best phrase for it:

“Not garbage disposal. Elemental reclamation.”

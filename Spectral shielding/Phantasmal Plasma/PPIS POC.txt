## Considerations & Limitations

* **Energy Cost**: Maintaining plasma in motion requires strong magnetic stabilization.
* **Signal Feedback Risk**: Improper calibration may cause interference with own systems.
* **Overheat Risk**: Thermal dumping required during prolonged stealth ops.
* **EMP Susceptibility**: Plasma may arc if not grounded during solar events.

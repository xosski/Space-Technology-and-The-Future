Engineering Challenges

There are several major hurdles that would need to be addressed before a concept like this could move beyond theory:

Maintaining stable plasma over long periods inside structural components.
Preventing plasma instabilities from coupling between neighboring cells.
Developing materials that tolerate continuous high temperatures and electromagnetic stress.
Synchronizing thousands of high-power inductive nodes with microsecond precision.
Demonstrating that the energy required to maintain the plasma network is justified by the performance gains.

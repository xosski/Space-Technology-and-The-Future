Lazarus Pulsewheel Core

A rotating maglev turbine ring where individually controlled electromagnetic nodes pulse in sequence around the circumference, sustaining rotor motion, smoothing power flow, and storing/releasing energy like a mechanical-electromagnetic heartbeat.

Think:

central turbine/flywheel = mass/momentum storage
circular maglev coils = “heart muscle”
timed EM pulses = contractions
neutral center bus = blood chamber / power manifold
Elohim cells = reserve organs
SCIL lattice = vascular system

The strong version is:

The reactor gives the first pulse.
The Pulsewheel preserves the motion.
The lattice catches the losses.
The ship spends power in heartbeats, not constant bleeding.

What it can plausibly do:

reduce peak reactor demand
smooth huge power spikes
store rotational energy
provide emergency burst power
stabilize shipwide EM circulation
act like a flywheel battery
recover braking energy back into the lattice

What it cannot do:

self-power forever
create net energy from magnetic attraction alone
beat without losses
replace the reactor entirely

Best framing:

A controlled electromagnetic flywheel that uses phased maglev pulses to maintain rotational energy and provide pulse-power stabilization across the SCIL reserve lattice.

Cooler names:

Lazarus Pulsewheel Core
Seraphim Heartwheel
Elohim Pulse Crown
The Maglev Myocardium
Crownwheel Induction Heart

My pick: Lazarus Pulsewheel Core. It sounds like it belongs in the GhostCore stack without getting too anime with a welding torch.

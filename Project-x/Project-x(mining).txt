Project: LEVITATION-X
(Localized Electromagnetic Vector Interference and Thermal Ionization via Oscillating Plasma Core Arrays)
Abstract

This paper introduces LEVITATION-X, an advanced application of the LEVITATION platform, repurposed as a non-contact, energy-based mining and material disintegration system. By leveraging the vector-stabilized core zone created by dual PTEC (Prismatic Tesseract Energy Core) arrays, LEVITATION-X introduces a superconducting chamber that amplifies rotational EM energy into a directed energy beam. This beam achieves precision mining by destabilizing atomic bonds through oscillating EM-field convergence and targeted ion discharge.

1. Conceptual Overview

Where LEVITATION created field stability for motion and suspension, LEVITATION-X focuses the same mechanics into concentrated thermal disruption. This is achieved by integrating a superconducting conduit between counter-oscillating PTECs, compressing the field vector and releasing it via a tuned magnetic aperture — essentially creating a scalable, steerable plasma-based laser.

This Proof of Concept outlines the architecture and advantages of the Tesla Coil Plasma-Polarity Engine (TCPPE)—a reactive energy infrastructure designed for spacecraft, vehicles, and exo-environments requiring on-demand charge manipulation, dual-polarity plasma handling, and dynamic armor amplification.

TCPPE enables bidirectional energy cycling through Tesla coil arrays embedded with plasma-lined conduits, forming an integrated feedback loop that hypercharges armor systems like Neon Genesis Armor and reduces external power dependencies.

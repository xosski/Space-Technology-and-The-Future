## CRITICAL PATH TO DEPLOYMENT

**If you do nothing else, do these four:**

1. **Communications Array Design** (6 weeks)
   - Design subspace transmitter architecture (or laser/conventional alternative)
   - Specify antenna placement, power requirements, transmission protocols
   - Build emergency beacon systems

2. **Electrical Distribution** (6 weeks)
   - Route power from Genesis Reactor to all ship systems
   - Design circuit protection, voltage regulation, fusing
   - Specify grounding architecture and redundant pathways

3. **Pressure Vessel Specification** (4 weeks)
   - Define hull geometry and dimensions
   - Certify pressure ratings, material specifications, safety factors
   - Design airlock/docking ring architecture

4. **Thermal Radiator Network** (4 weeks)
   - Design radiator panel placement
   - Calculate heat-rejection capacity vs. reactor output
   - Specify coolant circulation loops

**Why these four?** They unlock everything else. You cannot operate weapons, propulsion, or navigation until these are solved.
